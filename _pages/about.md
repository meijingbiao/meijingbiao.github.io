I am a PhD student at the [Machine Intelligence Laboratory (MIL)](https://mi.eng.cam.ac.uk/Main/), University of Cambridge, supervised by [Prof. Bill Byrne](https://mi.eng.cam.ac.uk/~wjb31/). My research focuses on **multimodal retrieval**, **hateful meme detection**, and **vision-language models**.

My work spans retrieval-augmented generation for visual question answering, content moderation with multimodal reasoning, and long-term personalized memory systems. I have published at top venues including NeurIPS, ACL, EMNLP, ICLR, and NAACL.

## Research Interests

- Multimodal Retrieval and Retrieval-Augmented Generation
- Hateful Meme Detection and Content Moderation
- Vision-Language Models
- Personalized AI and Long-Term Memory
